An Information Theoretic Analysis of Grid Cell Encoding

The full description of this work will be posted here after publicaiton, along with slides from previous presentations. This work was elevated to a podium talk at the Rice Neuroengineering Initiative Conference in 2022, and was presented as a poster at Society for Neuroscience in 2022.

In this work, we applied an information theoretic measure to grid cell firing in the rat entorhinal cortex, and analyzed how well that measure provided insights into the information content underlying those firing patterns. Stay tuned for updates, coming soon.
