Motivation

We wanted to explore which frequency band features may be the most useful in identifying motor intent in vECoG signals using the Stentrode. Additionally, we wanted to explore the potential somatotopy of these signals across the Stentrode.

Where this work has gone...

The paper describing this work has been accepted for publication in the Journal of Neural Engineering.
