What I've done, and where I want to be!

I’ve been a writer, first and foremost, for as long as I can remember. I spent a portion of the summer between my Junior and Senior years in high school attending Stanford’s Pre-Collegiate Summer Institutes for Creative Writing, during which I cultivated my skills in both poetry and short story writing. The way words sound moves me like nothing else, and compose an art form about which I am incredibly passionate. I have included a selection of my portfolio on this site, and do hope you enjoy reading them nearly as much as I enjoyed writing them.
